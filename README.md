## STRATEGIC BUSINESS MODULES

### 1. Vendor Performance Scorecard
Evaluates supplier reliability by analyzing delivery precision and quality. Provides a data-driven basis for vendor audits and contract negotiations.
- **Key SAP Metrics:** Delivery Date Variance & Quality Notification Rates.
- **Primary T-Codes:** ME2N, ME2L.

### 2. Cash Flow & Payment Terms ROI
A financial tool designed to calculate the liquidity impact of optimizing payment cycles. Demonstrates how procurement decisions influence corporate working capital.
- **Business Impact:** Capital Liquidity & Interest Savings.
- **Primary T-Code:** FBL1N.

### 3. Contract Compliance Analysis
Identifies "Maverick Buying" patterns - purchases made outside of negotiated master agreements. Quantifies financial leakage and enforces organizational discipline.
- **Business Impact:** Cost Reduction & Spend Transparency.
- **Primary T-Codes:** ME3L, ME3N.

### 4. Inventory Aging & Dead Stock Recovery
Identifies assets that have been stagnant for 180+ days. This analysis helps free up warehouse space and recovers capital for more productive use.
- **Business Impact:** Inventory Turnover & Warehouse Efficiency.
- **Primary T-Codes:** MB52, MC46.

### 5. Safety Stock & Reorder Point Planning
Determines the optimal buffer stock required to prevent production downtime while avoiding overstocking, based on lead-time volatility.
- **Business Impact:** Risk Mitigation & Supply Chain Continuity.
- **Primary T-Code:** MD04.
